What we make

Pindot Wear OS · Android

A bubble-popping game for your watch that gently distracts a baby or fussy toddler during diaper changes. The watch is worn on the caregiver's wrist; the screen is drawn upside-down so it reads upright to the child looking up. Settings are controlled from a companion phone app and sync to the watch in seconds.

Pindot started as a project to help my own 18-month-old son through the squirmy bit of a diaper change. It is free, it will stay free, and it does not collect any information about you or your child.
